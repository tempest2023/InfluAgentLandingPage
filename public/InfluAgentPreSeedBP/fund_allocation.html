Product Roadmap

Our initial focus is developing a fully functional AI social media agent optimized for Twitter (X), with a user-friendly dashboard providing AI-assisted growth insights. Starting with 50 users in Q1, we aim to reach 400 paying users by the end of year 1 through strategic growth initiatives.

Through strategic fund allocation and lean operations, we'll establish product-market fit, achieve consistent user growth with low churn (<5%), and scale to 2,000+ users and $4.36M ARR by year 2. This strong growth trajectory and proven unit economics will position InfluAgent favorably for a Series A round in the $15M-$25M range, enabling us to accelerate growth to 21,000 users and $45M ARR by year 5.
